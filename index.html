READY TO TAKE THE FIELD

The best defense is a good offense. In cybersecurity, staying proactive isn't optional — it's the only play that works. You're on offense, and the field is yours.

You have 10 modules to move the ball from the 10 yard line to the end zone. Each module puts a real cybersecurity threat in front of you — how it works, what it looks like, and what to do about it. Study the material, then answer 2 questions to advance 10 yards.

Get both right and you earn a First Down — the ball moves and you pick up a yard marker. Miss one and you're back to try again. You've got 3 downs to get it right before it's time to punt and reset.

10 10 modules, 10 yards each. Read the material, answer both questions correctly, and advance down the field.
3 3 downs per module. Each wrong attempt costs a down. Use all three and it's 4th down — punt and restart the module from the top.
